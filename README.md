## About The Project

A Tic Tac Toe game you can play in your browser made with vanila Javascript, HTML, and CSS3. 

Tic-tac-toe is game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner. [Wikipedia reference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tic-tac-toe)
